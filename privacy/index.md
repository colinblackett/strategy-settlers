# Privacy Policy for Strategy Settlers

**Effective date:** 5/25/2026

Strategy Settlers ("the app") is a companion tool for the board game
Settlers of Catan, This document
describes how the app handles your information.

## Data collected

None. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal
information whatsoever.

## Permissions

The app requests no Android permissions. It does not access:

- the internet
- your contacts
- your location
- the camera or microphone
- files on your device beyond its own assets
- any other device sensors or accounts

## Third parties

The app contains no advertising, no analytics, no tracking, and no
third-party SDKs. Your usage is not shared with anyone, because nothing
about your usage ever leaves the device.

## Local storage

The app stores a single preference on your device: which voice bank you
last selected. This is saved through standard Android `SharedPreferences`
and never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app clears it.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children, or from
any other user, because the app does not collect information at all.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
The effective date above will be updated to reflect the change.
